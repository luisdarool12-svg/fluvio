Circular initials avatar — uniform violet-light fill, violet-dark Syne initials. Used in list rows (34px) and the sidebar user (28px).

```jsx
<Avatar name="Sofía Mendoza" size={34} />
<Avatar name="María Fernanda Ríos" size={34} vip />
```

Initials come from the first two words of `name`. `vip` adds a coral ring.
