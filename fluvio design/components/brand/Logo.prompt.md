The Fluvio mark — three flowing wave currents that carry the brand from violet to coral without a CSS gradient. Mark alone, or the horizontal FLUVIO lockup.

```jsx
<Logo size={28} />                       {/* mark, light bg */}
<Logo size={28} wordmark />              {/* FLUVIO lockup */}
<Logo size={30} variant="mono" />        {/* white, dark sidebar */}
```

`variant`: `color` (violet/violet/coral) or `mono` (white at 100/65/90% per wave). Keep ~12–16px clear space around the lockup; never recolor individual waves.
